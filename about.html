About
me

Hi! My name is Anh Thuy Nguyen, but you can call me Thuy (Twee for pronunciation). I'm an Adelaide-based graphic designer and a software developer in the making.

I'm_
lycheebbltwee

Ever since I was a child, I loved everything to do with lychees—the fruit, flavoured drinks, anything. My favourite though, was lychee bubble tea. I have fond memories as a child following my mum to work and getting one of these as a treat.

So, I made this name for myself as a display name for social media, as well as some of my game handles. I recently decided to rebrand my logo to also reflect this.

I'm_
a learner

I have completed a Bachelor of Design (Communication Design) in 2019 at the University of South Australia. I've gained a vast amount of knowledge and skill through my time in and out of university.

I am very passionate to continue learning and expanding my skills and experience. This has led to work in various fields and as of right now, begin training as a developer with _nology.

I'm_
designed as me

"Designed as me" has been my tagline ever since establishing my professional identity as a graphic designer.

Why "as" and not "by"? Well, I believe that my life is made up of my identity, and the experiences that I have lived to get to where I am today. So in honouring that, I want to pour those things into my work; to create something that is sincere and meaningful.

I want to acknowledge that the work I've done is a captured moment of the person I was at that time.
